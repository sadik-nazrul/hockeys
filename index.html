Our Equipment
  • Skates: Properly fitted skates are crucial for performance and safety.
  • Sticks: Select sticks based on player preference, considering material, flex, and blade curve.
  • Protective Gear: Helmets, gloves, shin guards, and shoulder pads are essential for player safety.
Hockey Training
  • Fundamentals: Master basic skills like skating, passing, shooting, and puck control.
  • Strength and Conditioning: Develop off-ice fitness for endurance, speed, and agility.
  • Drills and Practice: Regularly engage in drills to refine skills and reinforce teamwork.
Private Lessons
  • Individualized Attention: Benefit from focused instruction tailored to your specific needs and skill level.
  • Personalized Feedback: Receive detailed feedback on your technique, strengths, and areas for improvement.
  • Customized Training Plan: Develop a training plan designed to address your goals and enhance your skills.
Ski Touring
  • Equipment Familiarization: Learn about ski touring equipment, including skis, boots, bindings, skins, and safety gear.
  • Technique Instruction: Master uphill climbing techniques, efficient stride patterns, and downhill skiing skills.
  • Route Planning: Understand how to plan routes, assess terrain, and navigate safely in backcountry environments.
Booking
  • Direct Booking: Reserve your ski tour directly through the tour operator's website or by contacting them via phone or email.
  • Online Travel Agencies: Use online platforms such as Expedia, Booking.com, or Airbnb to search for and book ski touring packages.
  • Tourist Information Centers: Visit local tourist information centers in your destination to inquire about available ski touring options and make bookings.
Pricings
  • Standard Package: Includes basic services such as guided tours, equipment rental, and transportation at a fixed price.
  • Customized Packages: Tailored to individual preferences, allowing you to select specific services like accommodations, meals, or additional activities.
